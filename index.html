About Pogiso Tours

Our History

Pogiso Tours was founded over a decade ago with a simple vision: to revolutionize corporate transportation by combining luxury, reliability, and professionalism. What started as a small family-owned operation has grown into a nationwide leader in corporate shuttle services. Our journey has been marked by consistent growth, unwavering commitment to excellence, and the trust of thousands of corporate clients who depend on us daily. We've invested in state-of-the-art technology, built a team of exceptional professionals, and expanded our fleet to serve clients across the entire country.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide exceptional corporate transportation solutions that exceed expectations while prioritizing safety, punctuality, and professionalism. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients by delivering consistent, reliable service every single time. Our core values—integrity, excellence, customer-centricity, and innovation—guide every decision we make. We're committed to continuous improvement, embracing new technologies, and fostering a culture where our team members take pride in delivering outstanding service that reflects our company's reputation and values.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Booking with Pogiso Tours is simple and convenient. You can book through our online portal at www.pogisostours.co.za/booking/, call our 24/7 call centre at your preferred time, or email us with your requirements. We'll confirm your booking within 2 hours and provide all necessary details about your shuttle service. For corporate accounts, we offer dedicated account managers to streamline the booking process.

We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made 24 hours or more in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% charge. Modifications to existing bookings can be made free of charge up to 12 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Our 24/7 call centre team is always available to assist with any changes. For corporate contracts with regular bookings, we offer flexible terms tailored to your specific needs.

Weekend and after-hours bookings may be subject to a small surcharge, which we'll clearly communicate during the booking process. Our night shift chauffeurs are specially trained to ensure your comfort and safety during all hours of operation.

Depending on the vehicle type, you may also enjoy leather upholstery, panoramic sunroofs, mini-bars, and entertainment systems. All vehicles are meticulously maintained and regularly detailed.
